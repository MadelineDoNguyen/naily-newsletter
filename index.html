EMBRACE THE BEAUTY OF NOVEMBER

This month, we're embracing cozy vibes and bold designs with a mix of warm, rich tones and edgy nail art trends.


Get ready for fresh inspiration and exciting looks to try this fall! As the leaves change and the air turns crisp, it’s the perfect time to experiment with colors and styles that reflect the beauty of the season.

From deep, velvety burgundies and moody greens to golden accents and intricate patterns, this month’s trends are all about making a statement.

Whether you are drawn to the elegance of minimalist designs or crave the drama of bold, artistic nails, we’ve got ideas to match every mood and occasion.

This issue is packed with inspiration to help you elevate your manicure game. Our spotlight this month shines on layered nail art, a trend that’s all about creating depth and dimension with creative overlays.

We’ll also dive into tips for maintaining healthy nails as the weather cools, because great nails start with great care. Plus, don’t miss our exclusive feature on the season’s most-requested nail shapes—hint: almond and stiletto are still reigning supreme!

As always, we love to see your creativity come to life. Be sure to tag us on social media with your fall nail designs, and you might just see your work featured in next month’s newsletter. Let’s make this season one to remember, one manicure at a time!
